Narcissus

The world is changing, ladies and gentlemen. Everything is becoming more obvious, even the issues we have. It is not a time to further hide them, but explore them, giving ourselves permission to see them, acknowledge them, accept them, embrace them, and surrender them to the ascended God we are that we may become even more real, fulfilled, and move closer to our potentials. In this show, we discuss one aspect of what we as individuals and as a society are doing to keep ourselves from moving. Since mental, emotional, physical, and spiritual movement is how God heals us and brings us more, non-movement is counterproductive to everything you want. Let's learn all about Narcissus, a great symbol for our times, whose challenge now reflects our own as we struggle with putting down the image we've created to be Who We Really Are.
